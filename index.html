What are most common problems of Air Conditioner?

  1. Filter is Not clean
  2. Thermostat is not working
  3. Refrigerant is leaking
  4. Breakers or Fuses not working
  5. Capacitors are not working
  6. Compressor problem
  7. Evaporator Coils & Condenser Coils are not working
  8. Worn Contactor problem

Common Mistakes in Refrigerator or Fridge

  1. Refrigerator not cooling.
  2. Refrigerator water dispenser not working.
  3. Refrigerator ice maker not working.
  4. Refrigerator ice dispenser not working.
  5. Refrigerator not defrosting.
  6. Refrigerator is noisy or loud.
  7. Refrigerator defrost drain clogged.
  8. Refrigerator freezer is cold, but refrigerator is warm.

Common Problems in a Washing Machine?

  1. The machine won't fill with water.
  2. The drum doesn't turn.
  3. The machine stops mid-cycle.
  4. The washing machine is noisy.
  5. Water not draining from washing machine.
  6. The washing machine won't spin.
  7. The door won't open.
  8. Clothes smell bad.
